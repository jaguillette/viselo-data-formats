Data Formats

Jeremy Guillette

I'm Jeremy Guillette, and before we get into the fun bits of visualization, I want to take some time to talk about data formats. You won't always have us to prepare nice datasets for you, so it's worth spending some time thinking about the ways in which how your data is formatted influence what you're able to do with it.

Data as Construct

Data is created. The production of a dataset requires choicess about what and how to collect and how to encode.

There is no such thing as "raw data"

Organizing a Chili Recipe

Unstructured Data

For this recipe you will need two pounds of ground beef, a chopped half onion, a teaspoon of ground black pepper, half a teaspoon of garlic salt, and two and a half cups of tomato sauce.

An organized list

  • 2 pounds ground beef
  • 1/2 onion, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic salt
  • 2 1/2 cups tomato sauce

"Flat" data model
